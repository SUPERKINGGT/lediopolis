Ledio Sinjari

As a computer science undergrad student at the University of Illinois at Chicago, I have had extensive experience with a wide variety of programming languages and methods including Microsoft’s .NET suite, C++, C#, and Python. I’ve worked on several semester-long projects that required flexibility, people skills, man and time management and constant learning. A few of my notable projects include; a Kinect driven interface in C# and XML that navigates, displays and visualizes accidents in public transportation across the country, a semester long two dimensional side scrolling video game that required working on a team of six students from different Universities, a photograph manipulation software written in C++ and the QT framework, and a Raspberry Pi powered security turret that used Python scripts to monitor an area for motion and sent out tweets of snapshots of intruders.
